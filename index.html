About Me

I'm Alok Mahato, a self-taught video editor from Nepal. I am using DaVinci Resolve Fusion with 3d Nodes.

I’ve been working with DaVinci Resolve Fusion for over 3 years, learning Resolve by myself through YouTube and online tutorials. I never attended any editing classes or film school — all my knowledge comes from consistent self-learning.

I believe that great editing is invisible. It's about rhythm, pace, and emotion, ensuring that the final product not only looks stunning but also resonates with its intended audience.
